History

The history of Area 51 can be traced back to about 100 years ago (probably not that long). It is rumored that it contains many lost technologies and undocumented beings.

A potential goldmine for technophiles and degenerates everywhere.

The fabled treasures of this fortfied temple have long been sought by many, yet all have come back empty-handed. Our goal is to change that, and be the first to breach the stronghold and show the world what truly exists down there.
